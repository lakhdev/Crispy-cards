The main page for the website will have banners with different images representing what we sell as well as bits of information such as benchmark tests advertising the products. I have decided to use ‘Adobe Arabic Regular’ Font because i believe it gives the website a bold look as well as make it look slick and finished. The website theme for all pages is a smooth black and grey background with a nvidia logo in the middle of it. This gives the website a finished and polished feel to it instead of having a plain white background which was the original idea. The images on the main page will have 3 images on it to make it look less cluttered while still grabbing the customers attention. Not cluttering the page with alot of information i believe was a good idea as it grabs the users attention and adding more pages and content will allow the website to have a more professional feel to it and make it more user friendly The product page has a very simple and sophisticated look. With information on each product in order of price range. Choosing the white font allows the user to easily see the information on the page and having the easy to look at grey background makes it even more easier to see. The informaiton of the products shows everything the customer will want to look at before purchasing. The white background over the images allows you to get a good look at the product, as for the font it is easy enough to see without a background and also looks better without one. The smallprint on the product details also makes the products look more realistic. This page is opened when a customer clicks on the product they are wanting to look at. It enlarges the image so the user can see it better, and also shows more product information. For most people who look at the product information this will be very useful, especially if you are going to buy the product every detail can come in handy. For example dimentions of the graphics card are shown to see if the product will fit in the customers computer. There is also an add to basket option which will take you to the basket page where you will be able to checkout and finish buying the product. This page shows the products you are wanting to buy from the website, it has a very simplistic style to it with alot of space around the information making it easy for the user to see. This means that there will hopefully be no mistakes in the products purchased. I believe it also makes the website look more professional and less like something that will scam you. There is also a proceed to checkout option where you will be able to finalise your sale. Finally there is a checkout page, where you will be able to input all your personal information, allowing the website to store this information in exchange for completing the order. The page looks very polished and professional with little to look at making it easy and simple to use. I have kept the theme of the website similar all the way through to the end, using the same style buttons and font so that it looks more elegant and professional
